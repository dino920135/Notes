- ## Radar Measurement Model
	- Given radar point $^R p_k$ in the radar frame $R$, decompose into two components:
		- $$^Rp_k=[r_k,\ \Omega_k]^T$$
	- range: $r_k \in R$
		- $$r^{gt}_k = r_k + \delta_{r_k}$$
	- azimuth, elevation: $\Omega_k \in S^2$
